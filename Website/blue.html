Research indicates that weightlifters demonstrate the ability to lift heavier weights in gyms with blue surroundings.

Blue is a remarkably soothing color that has the capacity to slow down metabolism, which is why it is rarely used by food brands in their branding. It boasts broad appeal, evident in its choice by global giants like Facebook and Twitter for their logos. Interestingly, blue tends to be more favored by men, with over 50% of male respondents in studies citing it as their favorite color.

Blue is often associated with:

  • Calmness
  • Tranquility
  • Stability
  • Loyalty
  • Faith
  • The concept of heaven
  • Loneliness
  • Sadness

Lavender

Purple playfully considers itself superior to all the other colors, but of course, it's all in jest ;) In reality, purple has a historical association with superiority, as it was favored by royalty to showcase their position and influence. It represents power, nobility, prestige, and opulence. The placement of purple on the color wheel can vary between warm and cool tones, depending on the balance between red and blue hues.

Purple is often linked with:

  • Royalty
  • Luxury
  • Sophistication
  • Magic
  • Spirituality
  • Moodiness
  • Superiority

Rose

After black and white, red is the initial color that babies are capable of perceiving.

Red, a color of great emotional and visual intensity, has the potential to physically impact individuals by increasing their metabolism, respiration, heart rate, and even triggering hunger. Moreover, its inherent attention-grabbing nature makes it a prevalent choice for the branding of various fast food chains.

Red is commonly linked with:

  • Courage
  • Power
  • Strength
  • Danger
  • Love
  • Passion
  • Romance

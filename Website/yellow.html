Yellow Room

Sunflower

In China, yellow is regarded as a color of good fortune and luck.

The essence of the sun radiates through yellow! It is a vibrant and invigorating color that sparks feelings of happiness and positivity. With its attention-grabbing nature, it serves as an excellent call to action, explaining why it's prevalent in warning signs and often combined with red in fast food logos.

However, it's worth noting that studies suggest that the color yellow can stimulate anxiety centers in the brain. Thus, it's wise to avoid painting your walls with a saturated yellow if you don't wish to encounter short tempers and crying babies.

Yellow is commonly associated with:

  • Sunshine
  • Playfulness
  • Fun
  • Happiness
  • Optimism
  • Caution
  • Anxiety
  • Cowardice

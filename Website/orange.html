Dahlia

During early Christian times, orange held symbolic significance, representing both glory and the abundance of fruits from the earth. It was also referred to as the "wisdom ray."

Orange, the milder sibling of red, exudes a less aggressive nature. While not as intense as red, it still maintains high visibility, making it a popular choice in construction and safety hunting equipment. Moreover, it serves effectively as a call to action. Orange has a vibrant and engaging personality.

Orange is commonly linked with:

  • Friendliness
  • Energy
  • Adventure
  • Enthusiasm
  • Creativity
  • Fun
  • A do-it-yourself (DIY) spirit
